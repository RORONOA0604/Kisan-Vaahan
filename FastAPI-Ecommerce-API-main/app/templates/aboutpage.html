About KISAN VAAHAN

Bringing farm-fresh organic produce directly to your doorstep since 2025

Fresh Market was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to connect local farmers with health-conscious consumers who value quality, freshness, and sustainability.

By partnering directly with local farmers and organic growers, we eliminate middlemen and ensure that you get the freshest fruits and vegetables at fair prices.

Today, we work with over 50+ certified organic farms and deliver to more than 10,000 households every week. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community support remains strong.
